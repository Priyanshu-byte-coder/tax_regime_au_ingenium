Long-Term Investing Tax Regime

Tax on Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG)

Tax Rate: 10% on LTCG exceeding ₹1 lakh in a financial year (No indexation benefit).

Holding Period: More than 1 year (for stocks).

Tax Component 10% on LTCG exceeding ₹1 lakh
Securities Transaction Tax (STT) 0.1% on the transaction value at the time of selling
